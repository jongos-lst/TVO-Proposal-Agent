# Getac B360 — Fully Rugged Laptop

## Overview
The Getac B360 is a 13.3-inch fully rugged laptop designed for demanding field environments. It combines powerful computing with military-grade durability, making it ideal for field service, utilities, oil & gas, and public safety applications.

## Key Differentiators
- **Dual Hot-Swappable Batteries**: Zero downtime battery replacement — workers never need to shut down or pause their work. This is unique in the 13.3" rugged category.
- **1400 nit Sunlight-Readable Display**: Industry-leading brightness for outdoor visibility. Competitors typically max at 1000 nit.
- **MIL-STD-810H & IP66**: Drop-tested to 6 feet, waterproof against high-pressure water jets. Operates in temperatures from -29°C to 63°C.
- **Bumper-to-Bumper Warranty**: Standard 3-year warranty covers accidental damage including drops, spills, and vehicle damage. Competitors typically exclude accidental damage.

## Performance Specifications
- Intel Core i7-1265U (12th Gen), up to 64GB RAM, up to 2TB SSD
- Thunderbolt 4 docking support for office flexibility
- Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2, optional 4G LTE/5G
- Built-in GPS, barcode reader, RFID reader options

## TCO Advantages
- 2% annual failure rate (industry average for non-rugged: 15%)
- Average device lifespan: 7+ years (vs 3-4 years for consumer/semi-rugged)
- Bumper-to-bumper warranty eliminates unexpected repair costs
- Hot-swap batteries save an estimated 15 minutes per battery change per worker per day
