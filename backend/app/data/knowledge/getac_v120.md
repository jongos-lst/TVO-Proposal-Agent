# Getac V120 — Fully Rugged Convertible Laptop

## Overview
The Getac V120 is a 12.2-inch fully rugged convertible laptop with a 360° hinge, designed for users who need both laptop and tablet modes in extreme field environments. AI-ready with Intel Core Ultra processors and Wi-Fi 7.

## Key Differentiators
- **360° Convertible Design**: Seamlessly switches between laptop, tablet, tent, and presentation modes for maximum versatility in the field.
- **AI-Ready**: Intel Core Ultra processors with AI Boost NPU for next-generation edge computing and AI workloads.
- **Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be)**: Latest wireless standard for fastest field connectivity.
- **Dual Hot-Swappable Batteries**: LifeSupport technology ensures zero-downtime battery replacement.
- **1000 nit LumiBond Display**: Sunlight-readable with auto-detecting touch (works with gloves, stylus, or bare fingers in rain).
- **IP66 + MIL-STD-810H + MIL-STD-461G**: Full military and environmental certifications.

## Performance Specifications
- Intel Core Ultra 7 265H vPro (up to 5.3GHz), Intel Arc 140V GPU
- Up to 64GB DDR5 RAM, up to 2TB PCIe NVMe SSD
- Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.4, optional 5G Sub-6
- Dual Thunderbolt 4, HDMI 2.0, dual USB 3.2

## TCO Advantages
- 2% annual failure rate
- Bumper-to-bumper warranty covers accidental damage
- Convertible form factor eliminates need for separate tablet and laptop
- Lightweight 2.1 kg — lightest in fully-rugged convertible class
